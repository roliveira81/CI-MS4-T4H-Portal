Reason for being...

When the passion took place

THE FOUR HORSEMEN werer born in 2002/2004 aiming to reproduce the spirit and presence of the most extraordinary band in the world, METALLICA. Born under the passion for their style and sound, our band's restless motivation is to bring the audience the feeling of being in an authentic Metallica concert

We do it not only by an accurate characterization and a thorough sound design but especially through the energy we discharge on stage. The influence of James Hetfield, Jason Newsted, Kirk Hammett and Lars Ulrich extends from what you hear to what you can see and feel.

That's why we care about intros, scenarios and even pyrotechnics - we want you to experience the heavy, the classic and the controversial in every album.

THE FOUR HORSEMEN is focused on represent that it is one of the best stages that METALLICA jhas lived in his career, "LIVE-SHIT" era. This time experienced by them from the years 1988 to 1993, where they bring the greatest classics in their "set list".

Among them, songs like: Enter Sandman, Nothing Else Matters, The Unforgiven, And Justice For All, Master of Puppets, Seek & Destroy, The Four Horsemen, Fade to Black, For Whom The Bell Tolls, Creeping Death and many others.
